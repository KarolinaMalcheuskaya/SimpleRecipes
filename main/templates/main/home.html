Chocolate oatmeal cookies

Mix oat flakes with eggs and sugar. Beat everything with a mixer. Then add flour, baking powder, soda, butter and cocoa - mix well.

Bake at 180 degrees for 20 minutes

We're going to need:

120 grams oat flakes

3 aggs

3-4 tablespoons of sugar

60 grams of butter

4 tablespoons flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

0.5 teaspoon soda

3 tablespoons cocoa

Сrepes

Fry in a hot pan.

We're going to need:

3 eggs

30 grams of sugar

30 grams of butter

200 grams of flour

Mannik

Mix semolina with kefir. Beat well. Add eggs - beat with a mixer. Let stand 5-7 minutes. Add butter, sugar, flour, baking powder - mix well

Bake for 30 minutes at 180 degrees

We're going to need:

3 eggs

100 grams of sugar

100 grams of vegetable oil

200 grams of semolina

200 grams of kefir

1 teaspoon baking powder

70 grams of flour
